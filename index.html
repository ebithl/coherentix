Who We Are

We are a technology company founded and led by seasoned engineers with over 150 years of combined experience in embedded products, software, and platform engineering.

  • Our team consists of skilled professionals and consultants specializing in both hardware and software engineering, with proven hardware and software product development across multiple industries including medical devices, healthcare solutions, industrial automation, networking, and operating systems.

  • We work at the confluence of Software, Hardware, Embedded Systems, Cloud, Data and Artificial Intelligence to develop Smart Intelligent products and solutions for various use cases of our customers.
